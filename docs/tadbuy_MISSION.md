# Tadbuy — Mission

## Our Purpose
To build the first truly sovereign advertising platform — where advertisers buy directly with Bitcoin and Lightning, creators earn in sats, and no intermediary takes a cut of attention or revenue.

## Core Values
1. **Bitcoin Native** — All transactions in BTC via Lightning Network.
2. **Nostr Integrated** — ZAP endpoints for direct creator payments.
3. **Privacy First** — No tracking pixels, no user surveillance.
4. **Geospatial Intelligence** — Target by region, not by identity.

## The Problem We Solve
Digital advertising is dominated by surveillance capitalism — platforms track users and take 20-40% of revenue.

## Our Solution
Geospatial ad-tech with Lightning invoices and Nostr ZAPs. Cross-tab unified state. No platform lock-in.

## Who It Serves
Bitcoin-accepting businesses, independent creators, Nostr users.
