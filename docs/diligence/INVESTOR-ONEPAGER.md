# Tadbuy — Investor / Partner One-Pager

**Live:** [https://tadbuy.giveabit.io](https://tadbuy.giveabit.io) · **GitHub:** [https://github.com/kitsboy/tadbuy](https://github.com/kitsboy/tadbuy) · **Version:** `5.0.17` · **Status:** LIVE

## One sentence
Bitcoin-native ad DSP — buy ads in sats, settle on Lightning.

## Problem
Ad payments are fiat-gated, slow, high-fee, and surveillance-heavy.

## Solution
DSP flows with Lightning/BOLT12 and Nostr ZAP distribution; geospatial targeting.

## Who it's for
Bitcoin businesses, independent publishers/creators, Nostr-native advertisers.

## Stage
Live frontend MVP — API/backend phases in progress

## Traction (honest)
Live SPA; rich docs; v5 line; Fedimint phase planned.

## Model / value flow
Ad platform economics in sats; minimize intermediary take; sovereign rails.

## Why Give A Bit
Part of an interlocking Bitcoin-sovereignty suite. Shared brand, Safe Harbour, open-source default, and cross-product rails (OTS, Lightning, Nostr) compound each product.

## 90-day north star (default)
Ship measurable usage on the live URL, close one integration or distribution partnership, and keep diligence docs green (this pack + SOURCE-OF-TRUTH).

## Risks & mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Backend availability vs static SPA | Phased M4 API; graceful frontend-only mode |
| Ad fraud / spam | Proof-of-payment + reputation layers |

## The ask (default — refine per conversation)
LN infra help, publisher pilots, ad-tech advisors.
